HOW IT WORKS

The ClearCurve Workflow

01

Nodal Ingestion Layer

Real-time multi-source ingestion from Reddit, Discord, TikTok, Instagram, Amazon, Google Trends, and proprietary APIs. Concepts are modeled as nodes in a dynamic knowledge graph.

02

Noise Filtration & Sentiment Reasoning

Transformer-based models distinguish flash fads from structural shifts. OpenAI-powered reasoning extracts the “Why” behind every trend (e.g., “Physical Security Anxiety” driving minimalist wallet searches).

03

Predictive Modeling Engine

Hybrid architecture: Time-series forecasting (Prophet + LSTM) + Transformer trend extrapolation. Outputs a proprietary Curve-Fidelity Score™ (0–100) with mutation predictions.

04

Automated Strategic Briefs

Generates actionable roadmaps for e-commerce launches, content strategies, and product pivots — complete with timelines, projected ROI, and competitive gap analysis.
